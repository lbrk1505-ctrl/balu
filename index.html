About the Mall

Where Lifestyle Meets Elegance

Star City Mall is more than just a retail complex; it's a vibrant destination nestled in the heart of the city. Spanning multi-tiered architectural designs, we host a selective directory of global brands, fine-dining restaurants, and dynamic amusement spaces.

Whether you are hunting for the latest runway fashion trends, picking up high-end gadgets, hosting a lunch, or watching a blockbuster film in our ultra-luxurious cinemas, we promise a stellar experience for everyone.

How to Find Us

Location & Arrival

Situated at the core Intersection of Star Boulevard and Highway 101, Star City Mall is easily accessible via car, metro, and city buses.

Physical Address

100 Mall Blvd, Star City, SC 12345

Parking Access

Gate A (North Boulevard) and Gate B (Express Highway). Undercover multi-level deck containing 4,000+ bays.

Public Transport

Star City Central MRT Subway Metro Station (Direct underground exit to Mall Level B1).
